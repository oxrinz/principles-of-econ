**What is the role of the financial system? Name and describe two markets that are part of the financial system in our economy. Name and describe two financial intermediaries.**
The role of the financial system is to allow the purchase of capital that would otherwise be too costly to obtain. This helps startups to get started, factories to be built, and houses to be built. The bond and the stock market are examples of financial markets. Banks and index funds are examples of financial intermediaries.

**What type of financial institution makes diversification easier in stocks and bonds?**
Index funds.

**What is investment? How is it related to national saving?**
Investment is the acquisition of capital. Saving is supplying the market for loanable funds.

**Describe a change in the tax code that might increase private saving. If this policy were implemented, how would it affect the market for loanable funds?**
Lowering the tax, or changing the income tax to a consumption tax. The supply of loanable funds would fall -> the interest rate would fall and the total amount of loanable funds would rise.

**What is a government budget deficit? How does it affect interest rates, investment, and economic growth?**
The demand for loanable funds would grow.
