**Why is the burden of a tax to taxpayers greater than the revenue recieved by the government?**
Deadweight loss.

**What is the concept of horizontal equity and why is it hard to apply?**
Horizontal equity is the idea that taxpayers of similar income should pay the same amounts of tax. It is hard to apply because income comes in different forms, and the different nuances are hard to balacne. Marriage tax is one example.
