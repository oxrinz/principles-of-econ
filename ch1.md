Water is necessary for life. Is the marginal benefit of a glass of water large or small?
Depends on the situation, is water is abundant, the benefit is small. If water is scarce, the benefit is large.

How are inflation and unemployment rate related in the short run?
The higher the inflation rate, the lower the unemployment rate. This is called the Philips Curve

Explain the two main causes of market failure and give an example of each.
Externality and market power. 
Externality is when the market fails to account for an external effect on the wellbeing of an economy's people. An example of it is pollution.
Market power is analogous with monopoly, if there is an economy where there is only one competitor while the cost of entry is high enough for nobody to be able to enter it, the competitor has a monopoly. It doesn't have the competitive incentives a normal market would have, and therefore it can rent seek, or lower quality of its products / services.
