**Describe the three attributes of monopolistic competition. How is monopolistic competition like monopoly? How is it like perfect competition?**
Free entry, many sellers, slightly different products.

**Does a monopolistic competitor produce too much or too little output compared to the most efficient level? What practical considerations make it difficult for policymakers to solve this problem?**
A monopolistic competitor produces too little output compared to the most efficient level. It is hard for policymakers to solve because upon any price regulations the companies would lose money. Whereas monopolies make profit, monopolistic competitors don't make profit, they have an equilibrium.

**How might advertising increase economic well-being?**
Advertising helps consumers make informed choices through seeing companies' willingness to advertise their product. Empirical evidence also shows that advertisement fosters competition and therefore lowers cost.
