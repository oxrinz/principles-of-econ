**What is commodity money? What is fiat money? What kind do we use?**
Commodity money has intrinsic money, fiat has none. We use fiat.

**If the fed wnats to increase the money supply with open-market operations, what does it do?**
It buys bonds from the government.

**What is the discount rate? What happens to the money supply when the Fed raises the discount rate?**
The discount rate is confusingly the interest rate that the fed has on other banks. A higher discount rate discourages banks from borrowing from the federal bank, therefore reducing the amount of money supply.
