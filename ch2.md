What is a production possibilities frontier or PPF?
PPF shows the maximum feasible combination of production of an entity or a nation.

Explain a production possibilities frontier for an economy that produces milk and cookies. What happens to this frontier if disease kills half of the economy's cow population?
The milk, as well as cookie frontier, if it depends on milk, will be reduced.

What is the difference between a positive and a normative statement?
A positive statement tells about how the world is, while a normative statement states how the would ought to be.
