**Give an example of a price ceiling and a price floor.**
Rent control and usa farming regulations in 1900s

**Which causes a shortage of a good - a price ceiling or a price floor? Which causes a surplus.**
Ceiling causes a shortage and floor causes a surplus.

**What mechanisms allocate resources when the price of a good is not allowed to bring supply and demand into equilibrium?**
Rationing, queues, discrimination, bribes. Tons of other things.

**What is the difference between a tax paid by the buyers and a tax paid by sellers?**
There is no difference.

**What determines how the burden of a tax is divided between buyers and sellers? Why?**
The ratio of demand and supply elasticity. If the supply elasticity is higher than the demand elasticity, the burden falls on the consumer. Inversely, if the supply elasticity is lower than the demand elasticity, the burden falls on the producer. The general rule is that whichever side is more inelastic bears the burden.

**What is the economists and politicians use for describing the distribution of tax burden?**
Tax incidence.
