**What is seasonal adjustment when calculating a nation's GDP?**
Consumption is typically higher on holidays, seasonal adjustment calculates GDP so that this fact doesn't make the value inconsistent over the time of the year.

**What is deapreciation?**
The deminishing value of inventory items.

**Describe the components of GDP.**
Consumption, investments, government spenditure, net exports.

**What is the difference between nominal GDP and real GDP.**
Real GDP is how many more items are produced, nominal GDP is just how much is created in terms of money.
